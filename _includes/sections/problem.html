Data-Rich, Insight-Poor?

Your Netbox Source of Truth is valuable. But are you getting the most from it?

Hours Wasted on Tracing?

Manually clicking through dozens of cables, patch panels, and devices to trace a single connection is slow, error-prone, and frustrating for your engineers.

Financial Data Disconnected?

Your asset data lives in Netbox, but financial data is in spreadsheets. Bridging this gap for depreciation reports and budget forecasting is a manual, laborious process.

Can't See the Big Picture?
