Get the Big Picture: Holistic Site Dashboards

Benefit: Make informed capacity and strategic decisions with a complete, aggregated view of any datacenter site. Stop clicking through dozens of racks and circuits to understand site health.

  • ✅ At-a-Glance KPIs: Instantly view key metrics for the entire site, including total racks, rack units, power capacity, asset value, and circuit counts.
